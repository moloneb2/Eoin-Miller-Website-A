Musical Background

Eoin Miller was the drummer for the group ‘My Albatross’ who were a Manchester based band whose sound was an eclectic, up-beat Indie/Rock style with complex vocals. They had their singles ‘Live and Live Lonely ', ‘Richman’s daughter’ along with the B sides of ‘I Don’t Love You’ and ‘Spending her Daisies’’ played on BBC and XFM radio during 2009 - 2010.

Current Projects

Eoin is finalising his debut album. He has been recently in two studios in Co Meath recording songs for his album. The first single 'Don't Be Lonely' is an acoustic upbeat ballad with poetic rhymes, released in December 2024. The second is called 'Talk' which is a tap along Ukulele led summer vibe tune. 'Unremittingly' is Eoin's 3rd single release. It's different from the others with more country/rockabilly tones. All singles are now available on all major streaming platforms along with an artistic fun and heart felt video on YouTube.
